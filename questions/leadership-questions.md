# Вопросы на Leadership

Банк вопросов для оценки leadership качеств, категоризированный по сигналам.

---

## 1. Initiative (Инициативность)

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about a time you went above and beyond what was expected.**
   - *Подсказка:* Конкретная инициатива, которую никто не просил
   - *Что показать:* Проактивность, ownership, impact

2. **Describe when you identified a problem and fixed it proactively.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как заметили проблему, что предприняли
   - *Что показать:* Observation skills, action bias

3. **Tell me about a time you proposed a new idea or process.**
   - *Подсказка:* От идеи до реализации
   - *Что показать:* Creativity, follow-through

4. **Describe a situation where you took ownership of something outside your role.**
   - *Подсказка:* Почему взяли на себя, как справились
   - *Что показать:* Broad ownership, team-first thinking

5. **How do you decide what to work on when there's no clear direction?**
   - *Подсказка:* Процесс приоритизации, alignment с целями
   - *Что показать:* Self-direction, strategic thinking

### Follow-up вопросы
- How did you get buy-in for your initiative?
- What if it had failed?
- How did you balance this with your regular responsibilities?

## 2. Influence Without Authority

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about a time you had to convince someone to change their mind.**
   - *Подсказка:* Какие аргументы использовали, как построили диалог
   - *Что показать:* Persuasion, data-driven approach

2. **Describe when you influenced a decision without having authority.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как получили влияние без позиции
   - *Что показать:* Soft skills, expertise-based influence

3. **How do you handle disagreements with more senior engineers?**
   - *Подсказка:* Respect + готовность отстаивать позицию
   - *Что показать:* Professional courage, diplomatic skills

4. **Tell me about a time you built consensus among disagreeing parties.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как нашли compromise, что сработало
   - *Что показать:* Mediation, finding common ground

5. **Describe when you had to push back on a request from leadership.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как аргументировали, результат
   - *Что показать:* Integrity, communication skills

### Follow-up вопросы
- What if you couldn't convince them?
- How did you prepare for the conversation?
- What would you do differently?

## 3. Mentorship & Growing Others

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about a time you helped someone improve their skills.**
   - *Подсказка:* Конкретный подход к обучению, результат
   - *Что показать:* Teaching ability, patience

2. **Describe how you onboarded a new team member.**
   - *Подсказка:* Структура onboarding, что создали
   - *Что показать:* Organization, empathy

3. **How do you give constructive feedback in code reviews?**
   - *Подсказка:* Примеры конструктивного фидбека
   - *Что показать:* Communication, teaching through review

4. **Tell me about a time you shared knowledge with your team.**
   - *Подсказка:* Tech talks, documentation, pairing
   - *Что показать:* Knowledge sharing, team investment

5. **Describe when you helped a struggling team member.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как идентифицировали проблему, подход
   - *Что показать:* Empathy, problem-solving

### Follow-up вопросы
- How did you know they needed help?
- What if they didn't improve?
- How do you adapt your teaching style?

## 4. Driving Results

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about your most challenging project. How did you deliver?**
   - *Подсказка:* Конкретные challenges и как преодолели
   - *Что показать:* Persistence, problem-solving

2. **Describe a time you had to deliver under tight deadline.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как приоритизировали, что сократили
   - *Что показать:* Focus, decision-making

3. **Tell me about a time a project was failing. How did you turn it around?**
   - *Подсказка:* Diagnosis + action plan
   - *Что показать:* Crisis management, leadership

4. **Describe when you had to coordinate work across multiple teams.**
   - *Подсказка:* Communication, alignment, dependencies
   - *Что показать:* Organizational skills, influence

5. **How do you handle blockers that are outside your control?**
   - *Подсказка:* Escalation, creative solutions
   - *Что показать:* Resourcefulness, communication

### Follow-up вопросы
- What would you have done if it still failed?
- How did you keep the team motivated?
- What was the key to success?

## 5. Decision Making

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about a difficult technical decision you made.**
   - *Подсказка:* Trade-offs, reasoning, outcome
   - *Что показать:* Analytical thinking, technical depth

2. **Describe when you had to make a decision with incomplete information.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как оценивали риски, что решило
   - *Что показать:* Comfort with ambiguity, judgment

3. **Tell me about a time you made a wrong decision. How did you handle it?**
   - *Подсказка:* Честное признание + recovery
   - *Что показать:* Accountability, adaptability

4. **How do you prioritize when everything seems urgent?**
   - *Подсказка:* Framework для приоритизации
   - *Что показать:* Strategic thinking, communication

5. **Describe a decision where you had to balance short-term and long-term goals.**
   - *Подсказка:* Trade-off analysis, stakeholder management
   - *Что показать:* Strategic thinking, business acumen

### Follow-up вопросы
- What alternatives did you consider?
- How did you get buy-in for your decision?
- What would you do differently now?

## Вопросы про конфликты и сложные ситуации

### Основные вопросы

1. **Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a coworker.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как разрешили, что узнали
   - *Что показать:* Conflict resolution, maturity

2. **Describe when you disagreed with your manager's decision.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как выразили несогласие, результат
   - *Что показать:* Professional courage, respect

3. **Tell me about a time you had to give negative feedback.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как подготовились, delivery
   - *Что показать:* Communication, empathy

4. **Describe a situation where you had to manage competing priorities.**
   - *Подсказка:* Как балансировали, коммуницировали
   - *Что показать:* Prioritization, transparency

5. **How do you handle a situation where you're blocked by another team?**
   - *Подсказка:* Escalation path, relationship building
   - *Что показать:* Problem-solving, collaboration

### Follow-up вопросы
- How did the other person react?
- What would you do if it happened again?
- How did this affect your relationship?

## Situational/Hypothetical вопросы

Эти вопросы проверяют ваш подход, а не прошлый опыт:

1. **What would you do if you discovered a critical bug right before release?**

2. **How would you handle a team member who consistently misses deadlines?**

3. **What would you do if you disagreed with the technical direction set by a staff engineer?**

4. **How would you approach a project with unclear requirements?**

5. **What would you do if you realized your team's approach was fundamentally flawed?**

### Как отвечать на situational вопросы
1. Уточните контекст при необходимости
2. Опишите ваш подход пошагово
3. Объясните reasoning за каждым шагом
4. Если есть похожий реальный опыт — приведите его

## Leadership на разных уровнях

### Для L3-L4 (Junior/Mid)

**Фокус вопросов:**
- Learning и growth
- Инициатива в рамках своих задач
- Помощь команде

**Типичные вопросы:**
- "Tell me about a time you learned something new quickly"
- "How do you ask for help when stuck?"
- "Describe when you improved your own workflow"

### Для L5 (Senior)

**Фокус вопросов:**
- Influence на команду
- Technical leadership
- Mentoring

**Типичные вопросы:**
- "Tell me about a design decision you drove"
- "How do you mentor junior engineers?"
- "Describe leading a cross-functional project"

### Для L6+ (Staff+)

**Фокус вопросов:**
- Organizational impact
- Strategy и vision
- Building systems and processes

**Типичные вопросы:**
- "Tell me about changing engineering culture"
- "How do you influence technical direction across teams?"
- "Describe building a new team or capability"
