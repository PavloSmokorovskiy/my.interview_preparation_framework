## По категориям вопросов

### Conflict / Disagreement

**Вопросы:**
- "Tell me about a conflict with a coworker"
- "How do you handle disagreements with senior engineers?"
- "Describe a time you had to convince someone"

**Рекомендация:** #1 Conflict (primary), #2 Leadership (backup)

**Акцент:** Humility (понял его concerns), Influence (данные, не эмоции), Collaboration (win-win решение)

---

### Failure / Mistake

**Вопросы:**
- "Tell me about your biggest professional mistake"
- "Describe a time you failed"
- "Tell me about critical feedback you received"
- "Tell me about a project that failed"
- "Describe a time you wasted effort"

**Рекомендация:**
- #3 Failure (technical mistake) — для вопросов о errors, incidents
- #6 Failure backup (wrong direction) — для вопросов о wrong decisions, wasted effort

**Акцент:** Ownership (моя вина, не blame), Learning (что изменилось), Recovery (как исправил)

**Важно:** НЕ выбирай "fake failure" — это red flag!

---

### Leadership / Initiative

**Вопросы:**
- "Tell me about leading without authority"
- "Describe when you took initiative"
- "Tell me about a time you went above and beyond"

**Рекомендация:** #2 Leadership (primary), #5 Achievement (backup)

**Акцент:** Initiative (сам начал), Influence (убедил без власти), Results (измеримый outcome)

---

### Mentorship / Helping Others Grow

**Вопросы:**
- "Tell me about helping someone improve their skills"
- "Describe when you mentored a teammate"
- "How do you help junior engineers grow?"
- "Tell me about onboarding a new team member"

**Рекомендация:** #2 Leadership (primary) — теперь включает mentorship компонент

**Акцент:** Teaching (не просто делал за них), Enabling (помог стать самостоятельным), Scale (один стал champion и учил других)

---

### Ambiguity / Uncertainty

**Вопросы:**
- "How do you handle ambiguity?"
- "Describe a project with unclear requirements"
- "Decision with incomplete information"

**Рекомендация:** #4 Ambiguity (primary), #5 Achievement (backup)

**Акцент:** Framework (reversible/irreversible), Bias for Action (не ждал), Iteration (feedback loops)

---

### Achievement / Impact

**Вопросы:**
- "What are you most proud of?"
- "Tell me about significant impact"
- "Biggest achievement"

**Рекомендация:** #5 Achievement (primary), #2 Leadership (backup)

**Акцент:** Quantified results ($200K), Self-initiated (не назначено), Lasting impact (процессы)

---

### Technical Decisions

**Вопросы:**
- "Difficult technical decision"
- "Build vs buy"
- "How do you make decisions?"

**Рекомендация:** #4 Ambiguity (primary), #1 Conflict (backup)

**Акцент:** Trade-offs analysis, Data-driven, Stakeholder buy-in

## Quick Reference Card

```
CONFLICT / DISAGREE      → #1 Conflict
FAILURE / MISTAKE        → #3 Failure или #6 (backup)
LEADERSHIP / INITIATIVE  → #2 Leadership
MENTORSHIP / HELPING     → #2 Leadership
AMBIGUITY / UNCERTAINTY  → #4 Ambiguity
ACHIEVEMENT / IMPACT     → #5 Achievement
CONVINCING OTHERS        → #1 Conflict, #2 Leadership
PRESSURE / DEADLINE      → #3 Failure, #4 Ambiguity
TECHNICAL DECISION       → #4 Ambiguity, #1 Conflict
WRONG DIRECTION          → #6 Failure (backup)
```

## Адаптация истории под вопрос

### Пример: История #1 (Conflict) для разных вопросов

**"Tell me about a conflict"**
> Акцент на разногласие и его разрешение

**"How do you convince someone?"**
> Акцент на процесс убеждения: данные → понимание concerns → компромисс

**"How do you handle disagreements with senior engineers?"**
> Акцент на уважение к опыту + data-driven подход

---

### Пример: История #5 (Achievement) для разных вопросов

**"What are you most proud of?"**
> Полная история с emphasis на impact

**"Tell me about taking initiative"**
> Акцент на "это не было моей задачей, но я увидел проблему"

**"Describe a time you went above and beyond"**
> Акцент на "в своё время" и "beyond my role"

---

## Сценарий: Что если вопрос не подходит ни к одной истории?

1. **Попроси уточнить** — "Could you give me more context on what you're looking for?"

2. **Адаптируй ближайшую историю** — Большинство историй покрывают несколько черт

3. **Будь честен** — "I don't have a perfect example, but let me share a related situation..."
